Lesson 1: Lyrics

Actions items for you and your playlists

Looking Through Your Playlist

People say that you can tell a lot about someone by looking at their playlist. What does yours tell you about yourself?

Self-reflection is an important skill in all areas of life but it can also be applied to your playlist. If you analyzed the lyrics you are hearing, do they carry what you want to say about your life or where you want to go? What are the songs you listen to the most? What are they telling you? Dig deeper into where the lyrics are taking you and consider making a few changes so the soundtrack of your life is a good one.

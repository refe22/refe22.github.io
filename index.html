What is Film Anaylsis?

Lights.Camera.Action!

Have you ever been in a movie theatre and you are thinking, How on earth did they do all of that? From specific effects to killing your favorite character, film students analyze the how, what, and why, that gets The Avengers: Endgame to be so exciting and moving and The Notebook to be so sad and memorable. On thing about film is that anyone, without study can tell if a movie is awful, but not everyone knows why. Follow us at The Unoffical Film Student Blog to get into the details on what makes a movie worth watching.

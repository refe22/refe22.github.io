Music is a Journey

Music is everywhere!

With modern technology, people can have a 24/7 soundtrack playing in their ears just the way they want it. Music is an industry that only keeps evolving and is never out of style. From country music to heavy metal, there is always someone tuned in.

But what are we tuned into?

Even though the beat might be "killer" the message behind the song might not be so great. For every art form there is a purpose or intent behind it. Even music without lyrics seem to have a spirit in them that moves you without telling you "this is sad" or "this is happy." What is passing through our ears and into our minds has emotion and meaning behind it. So, what is that meaning?

What's in the Lyrics?

From the Bible, Romans 10:17 says:

"So then faith comes by hearing, and hearing by the word of God."

But what does that have to do with music?

A lot! Here's the thing. That stuff we are putting in our ears, it feeds into how we view things or think about things. We go to school to learn by watching but also listening to what our instructor is telling us.

"So if I listen to music about being a gang member I will become one?"

No. What I am saying is that if you constantly listen to music that promotes the idea that worth comes from making a lot of money or dressing/acting a certain way will get into a relationship, it will start affecting how you view those subjects and change the way you think about them.

Dancing Time!

So what about that song with the killer beat but not so good message?

The bad news is you probably should let that one gone. You will be better off not listening to that strong. The good news is that a great instrumental and awesome lyrics do not have to be a trade off. There are so many artists out there and not every song by one artist is bad but not every song by your favorite artist is neccessarily good either. Remember, music is expression which means it comes out of different viewpoints and experiences. However, with all the easy ways you can find music, there are so many artists and songs just waiting for you to discover them. Don't put your dancing shoes away just yet!
